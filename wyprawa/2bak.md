## [EN] Modification Details  
Installation of a secondary 60-liter fuel tank from a Fiat Doblo II. The tank is mounted underneath the vehicle, replacing the original spare wheel carrier.  

### Key features:  
* **Transfer System:** Fuel is transferred to the main tank using the original Doblo fuel pump, activated by a switch in the cabin.  
* **Filler Neck:** A separate filler neck was fabricated, requiring modification of the main filler pipe and cutting an access hole in the interior panel and body (caution: mind the ECU near the seatbelt mechanism!).  
* **Protection:** The tank is protected by a 4mm aluminum skid plate, which also acts as a heat shield from the exhaust side.
